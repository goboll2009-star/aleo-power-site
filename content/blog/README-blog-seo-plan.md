# WEUP B2B Blog SEO / GEO / AIO Content Plan

## Source Context

- Business: B2B supplier of Chinese herbal raw materials, botanical extracts and TCM ingredients.
- Target Buyers: Importers, distributors, supplement brands, herbal product brands, wholesalers, TCM product buyers and OEM/ODM customers.
- Target Markets: North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, Middle East, Australia and selected Asian markets.
- Website Goal: Generate qualified RFQs, build trust, support Google SEO, GEO, AIO and paid traffic landing paths.
- Content Principle: Avoid consumer medical claims. Focus on procurement, quality control, documentation, packaging, MOQ, supplier selection and export preparation.

## 4. Image Planning Summary

Each article includes 4 AI image briefs covering:

- Hero / product scene
- Product detail or specification photo
- Factory testing / lab testing photo
- Packaging / shipment inspection photo

Style requirements:

- Realistic B2B photography
- WEUP white and logo-green branding
- Clean factory, lab or export warehouse environment
- No exaggerated medical or consumer wellness claims
- Suitable for Google image SEO and AI search citation context

## 5. CTA / Popup Summary

Each article includes:

- One mid-article CTA after the main buyer education section.
- One end-of-article CTA for RFQ or catalog request.
- Popup trigger plan:
  - Scroll to 40%
  - Stay 30 seconds
  - Exit intent
- Required fields:
  - Name
  - Email
  - Phone
- Optional fields:
  - Company
  - Country
  - Product Requirement
  - Message

## 6. Publishing Priority Recommendation

### Publish First: Article 1
Reason: It captures the broadest B2B sourcing intent and builds the foundation for Chinese herbal raw materials, slices and wholesale supply. It should link strongly to product category pages and the inquiry form.

### Publish Second: Article 2
Reason: It supports higher expertise signals for botanical extracts, COA, testing and quality control. This is especially useful for AI Overview/GEO because the content answers specific procurement questions.

### Publish Third: Article 3
Reason: It has strong conversion value for private label and OEM buyers, but the audience is narrower. Publish after the first two articles create trust and topical coverage.
